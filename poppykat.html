Poppy Kat

Pop into this cheerful Cheras cafe if you are a cat lover!
Poppy Kat serves you with their scrumptious croffles as well as some adorable company while you are there.

Features

The Foods

Poppy Kat specializes in croffles and pastries. If you are a fan of croffles, hit this cafe right away!

The Drinks

Don't forget to get yourself some of their in-house refreshing drinks and coffees while enjoying your croffles.

The Cafe

'Come for the croffles, stay for the cats', who would not love for some furry friends' company, eh?
