Rollerwa

Have you ever tried rollerskating? If you haven’t, it’s about time that you do! LET‘S ROLL IN ROLLERWA!

Features

Instagrammable Spots

Enjoy taking cool insta pics in Rollerwa with its decorated hallways with disco balls (it’s a mirror hallway!) and hanging lights that’ll lead you to a more insta-worthy space.Also,Rollerwa have cute and aesthetic backgrounds such as the angel wing background, the photobooth space with mirrors, and many more background with neon writings on the walls.

Take a break

Feel tired from all those fun skating? Take a snack break at Rollerwa Disco Cafe with plenty of drinks and snacks to choose from

Merchandise and Goods

Enjoy Rollerwa? There is a section that displays Rollerwakl’s own merchandise ranging from hoodies, t-shirts, roller skates, and safety gears. Take some merchandise home with you!
